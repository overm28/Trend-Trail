How To Play
Grow your trend as much as you can by the end of the game and win by finishing with the most followers.
  • Each player draws 3 random trend options and 3 random starting-following options, then picks 1 of each.
  • Roll the dice on your turn to move forward across the board.
  • Boost spaces help your trend rise, flop spaces make you lose followers, and trend twists can move you forward or backward.
  • When you land on an Ad Card space, your dice roll becomes your base followers, then the ad wheel multiplier decides how many followers you gain.
  • The player with the most followers when the game ends wins.
This is a game of luck, much like trends in real life, may the luckiest trend win.
